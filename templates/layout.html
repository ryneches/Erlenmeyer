About the Sample Registrar

The Sample Registrar was created by Russell Neches as part of the Project MERCCURI collaboration among UC Davis, Science Cheerleader, Scistarter, Sace Florida and NanoRacks.

The Sample Registrar is designed to be a central clearinghouse for coordinating citizen science sample collection and return.
